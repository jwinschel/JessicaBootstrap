About Ticket Collector

Barcodes are placed on each delivery ticket. Upon delivery, barcodes are scanned using the Ticket Collector app. All deliveries are instantly logged and categorized providing benefits to the supplier, the contractor, and the owner:

Automatically log delivery times, quantities, and drop-off locations with the scan of a barcode.

Significantly simplify the process of assigning deliveries to cost codes and pay items by giving foreman and inspectors a filtered list to select from on the spot.

Customize to your job and preferences.

Eliminate the need to track quantities.
